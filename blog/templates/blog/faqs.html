What is Lincart?
Lincart is a web app, where a student sell his product to another student directly without involving of any other third person.
How can a buyer contact the seller?
The buyer who needs to buy a product, click the button "Contact" of its corresponding product, and then both can further chat in ?notifications/Messages Section to move further and meet.
How to enter the product details in lincart?
Go to "Sell Product" section in menu and enter the required details of product and click submit. And you can further edit the details in "Your Products" section, click the three dots of the corresponding product and the click edit, there you can edit the details of the your product which you want to sell.
